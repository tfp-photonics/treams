treams.TMatrixC.xw_sca_avg¶

property TMatrixC.xw_sca_avg¶

Rotation and polarization averaged scattering cross width.

The average is calculated as

\[\langle \lambda_\mathrm{sca} \rangle = \frac{2 \pi}{n_{k_z}} \sum_{sk_zm} \sum_{s'{k_z}'m'} \frac{|T_{sk_zm,s'{k_z}'m'}|^2}{k_s}\]

where \(k_s\) is the wave number in the embedding medium for the polarization \(s\). and \(n_{k_z}\) is the number of wave components \(k_z\) included in the T-matrix. The average is taken over all given z-components of the wave vector and rotations around the z-axis. It is only implemented for global T-matrices.

Returns:

float or complex
