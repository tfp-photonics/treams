Spherical Hankel function of the first kind

Defined by

\[h_n^{(1)}(z) = \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2z}} H_{n + \frac{1}{2}}^{(1)}(z)\]

where \(H_{n + \frac{1}{2}}^{(1)}\) is the Hankel function of the first kind.
