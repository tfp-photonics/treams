ptsa.SMatrix.field_outside¶

SMatrix.field_outside(illu)¶

Field coefficients above and below the S-matrix

Given an illumination defined by the coefficients of each incoming mode calculate the coefficients for the outgoing field above and below the S-matrix.

Parameters:

illu (tuple) – A 2-tuple of arrays, with the entries corresponding to upwards and downwards incoming modes.

Returns:

tuple
